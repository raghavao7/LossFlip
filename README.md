# LossFlip

LossFlip---"Don’t lose it. Flip it."
something is better than 0
Core Mission:
To help users — especially students and hostellers — save money, avoid financial loss,
and make smarter purchases by centralizing deals, enabling resale of unused tickets and
last minute deals, and adding secure escrow-based peer transactions.

Target Audience:
🧑‍🎓 Students & Hostellers — cost-conscious, often dealing with canceled plans or impulse buys,goes to shopping

✈️ Travelers — holding onto last-minute tickets they can’t use,more cancellation fee

🎉 Concert/Event-goers — plans change, tickets shouldn’t go to waste

🎁 Deal Seekers — looking for gift cards, vouchers, or cashback

🛍️ Smart Shoppers — want to spend, but wisely,before buying something(for smarter approaches)

{Negotiation happen on the deal price{buyer can offer and seller can decide}}

🔐 Trust & Safety Measures
✅ OTP/email verified user registration

🔒 Escrow-based payment hold

⚠️ Flag/report mechanism for buyers/sellers

📄 Digital proof requirement for disputes

🚨 Rate limits, bot detection, and CSA escalation

🧾 Revenue Model
🪙 1–1.5% transaction fee on both sides

🎯 Promoted deals from brands & voucher sellers

💎 Verified Seller Premium Badge

🧠 In-app promotions for partner platforms

🎓 Campus Ambassador referrals & campaigns

💬 Customer Support Bot Handles FAQs, auto-flags complex issues
buyer and seller can communicate through chat
🎥 Proof Handling Screen recording/upload required for certain claims
🧾 Top-Bar Announcements Alerts, updates, featured deals on web/app

👨‍💼 Admin Dashboard Moderate users, flag reports, post announcements

📊 Spending Insights (Optional) Track personal savings or missed losses
